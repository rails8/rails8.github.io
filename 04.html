We specify our coding style and let our comments generate documentation:

  1. ▶︎ Adapt .rubocop.yml to your coding style.
  2. ▶︎ Add yard to the Gemfile (and doc/ to .gitignore).
  3. ▶︎ Add yard-style comments on top of every public method and class.
  4. ▶︎ Include coding style and 100% documentation check in bin/ci.
